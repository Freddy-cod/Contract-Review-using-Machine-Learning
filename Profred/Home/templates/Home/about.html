About

Athena leverages AI and data to make the contracting process easy and fast .

Drafting

Athena analyses data to give you a template with the most favourable terms of a contract . You can then edit the clause options to suit your business needs. Athena generates questions to help you personalise the template . Every response to the question will result in the deletion or addition of a clause .

AI Contract Review

Athena uses natural language processing to flag legal terms and clauses, then help you draw your attention to their implications .It then provides you with alternative terms and clauses than are more favourables.

Contract Management

Athena provides a platform for collaborative writing and editing . The platform allows the parties to the contract to negotiate the terms of the contract through flagging and feedback tools in real time. The platform accelerate conclusion of a contract through its e-signing tool.
